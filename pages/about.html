About ToolsHub

What is ToolsHub?

ToolsHub is a collection of free online calculators and utility tools designed specifically for India. Whether you need to calculate your monthly loan EMI, plan a SIP investment, estimate your income tax, figure out your electricity bill, or check GST amounts, ToolsHub has you covered.

All our calculators run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter. Your privacy is fully respected — every calculation happens locally on your device, with zero data sent to any server.

What We Offer

ToolsHub currently offers 17 free calculators spanning personal finance, taxation, and everyday utility needs:

  • Finance: EMI, SIP, FD, PPF, Compound Interest, Loan Eligibility
  • Tax: Income Tax, GST, TDS, HRA Exemption, Gratuity
  • Bills & Lifestyle: Electricity Bill, Salary (CTC to In-Hand), Rent Affordability, BMI, Age, Percentage

Each tool is built to be fast, accurate, and mobile-friendly — no sign-ups, no fees, no clutter.

Who Built This?

ToolsHub is built and maintained by Akib Saify, a compliance professional based in Delhi, India. ToolsHub is part of the akibsaify.in project ecosystem — a personal initiative to build useful, accessible tools for everyday Indians.
