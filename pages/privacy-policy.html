Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

ToolsHub (tools.0110059.xyz) is a free utility tools website operated by Akib Saify from Delhi, India. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

ToolsHub is a static website. Our calculators and tools run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our calculators. All calculations happen locally on your device.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. This may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (city/country level)
  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is aggregated and anonymised. We cannot identify individual users from analytics data.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Affiliate Links

Some pages contain affiliate links to Amazon.in through the Amazon Associates India programme. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Affiliate links are clearly marked and use rel="nofollow sponsored" attributes.

Cookies

ToolsHub itself does not set any cookies. However, third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set cookies on your browser. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — display advertising
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate product recommendations
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS and security

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS. Since we don't collect personal data through our tools, there is minimal risk of data exposure. All calculations are performed client-side in your browser.

Children's Privacy

ToolsHub is a general-purpose utility website. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: akibsaify [at] gmail.com
